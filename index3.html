This college has been my first option for a while after Katelyn Tuhoy won the 2018 NCAA D1 Cross Country Championships. Florida State University

I also like FSU for its running program! Some very famous runners have come from the program, with the most prominent being Parker Valby! I know Florida is a long way from Chicago, but I would still like to put this as an option because of its just amazing running program!
